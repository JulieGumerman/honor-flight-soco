It is with great sadness we must announce that Honor Fight of Southern Colorado will no longer be conducting Honor Flights.

The current situation has prevented is from doing any fundraising and we are not in a position to conduct our October flight (if restrictions from the national organization are even lifted by then). For Colorado veterans interested in travelling with one of the other two hubs in Colorado, please contact Rocky Mountain Honor Flight or High Plains Honor Flight.

We would like to thank all the volunteers, guardians, and donors who enabled us to make 13 Flights, creating so many wonderful memories for the HEROES who served in WWII, Korea, and Vietnam. It was our distinct honor to have shared this experience with y'all.
